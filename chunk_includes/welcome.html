Welcome!

Thank you for participating in this study!


In this study, you will be asked to listen to someone read a children's story! The story is roughly 30 minutes long. Your task is to hit the Space bar whenever there is a word that begins with the sound "T".

You will start by listening to the introduction of the story to practice. There are FIVE words that start with a "T" sound in this practice. See if you can get them all!


Some tips and reminders:
  • Some words will be tricky because they start with the LETTER "T" but not the SOUND "T" like the word "THE"
  • Do not press Space for words that start with the sound "TH"
  • Respond as quickly as you can. It helps to rest your thumbs on the Space bar in anticipation.
  • You will not be able to pause the story after starting! So please give yourself 30-40 minutes of uninterrupted time!
  • At the end of the audio, just type the last word that you heard in order to continue with the study.

Comprehension Check:

 1. What is your task?



2. What do you do at the end of any audio?



3. How many words will start with a "T" sound in the practice introduction audio?



4. Is your screen in Full screen mode?



5. Is your volume turned up sufficiently? (We recommend using headphones/ear buds, if available)

Then press Enter to continue!
