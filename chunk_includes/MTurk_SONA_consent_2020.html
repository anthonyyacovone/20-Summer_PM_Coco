Study Information!

Please read this consent agreement carefully before deciding whether to participate in this study.

Purpose of the research:

To better understand how and what people attend to stories when listening to stories.

What you will do in this research:

In this experiment, you will listen to someone read a children's book to you. Your task is to press the 'Space' bar whenever there is a word that begins with the sound "T" like "today" or "turkey." You will be asked comprehension questions at the end of the story. This task is estimated to take about 40 minutes based on previous participants' reported times; however, it may take longer.

Risks:

There are no anticipated risks associated with participating in this study.

Benefits:

There are no anticipated benefits to the participant beyond whatever amusement you happen to derive from the task.

Selection of participants:

To participate in this study, you must be 18 years of age and a native speaker of English.

Compensation:

Your compensation for completing this task will be $7.00 or 1 course credit (for university students). This is what you agreed to before accepting to participate in this study.

Confidentiality:

Your participation in this study will remain confidential. We do not have access to your identity.

Participation and withdrawal:

Your participation in this study is completely voluntary, and you may quit at any time without penalty or loss of benefit to which you may otherwise be entitled.
